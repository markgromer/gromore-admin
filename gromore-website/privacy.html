Privacy Policy

How GroMore Media collects, uses, stores, and deletes data across reporting, advertising, and messaging workflows.

GroMore Media provides reporting, advertising operations, lead management, and messaging automation software for business clients. This Privacy Policy explains what information we process, why we process it, and how users can request access, correction, or deletion.

Information We Process

Depending on enabled features, GroMore may process platform account information, campaign and analytics data, lead contact details, message transcripts, uploaded creative assets, and client workspace settings needed to deliver the service.

How We Use Data

We use connected data to generate reports, monitor performance, manage inbound leads, automate qualifying conversations, export materials to connected storage destinations, and support troubleshooting. We do not sell connected platform data.

Sharing and Retention

Data is shared only with providers and integrations needed to operate the service. We retain data only as long as reasonably necessary for service delivery, compliance, dispute resolution, and account recovery.

Requests and Contact

To request access, correction, export, or deletion, email privacy@gromoremedia.com.
