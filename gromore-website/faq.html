FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers to the things people actually ask. If your question is not here, reach out and we will respond within a business day.

General

GroMore connects to your Google Ads, Facebook Ads, GA4, Search Console, and Google Drive accounts. It pulls your performance data, generates month-over-month reports with AI-powered recommendations, and gives you tools to plan and launch campaigns, schedule Facebook posts, and track every change through a full audit trail. It is a single system that covers reporting, insights, and execution.

No. GroMore is a platform, not an agency. We do not manage your campaigns for you. We provide the reporting, recommendations, and tools so you can manage them yourself, or with minimal help. Think of it as having an experienced marketing analyst + campaign manager available 24/7, but you stay in control.

No. The system generates reports in plain English and provides specific, prioritized recommendations. When the report says "your cost per lead for emergency plumbing went up 22% because competitor X started bidding on your brand keywords," it also says exactly what to do about it. You do not need to understand Google Ads terminology.

Local service businesses that generate leads through Google and Facebook advertising. Roofing, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, cleaning services, landscaping, pest control, and similar trades. The campaign templates, reporting, and recommendations are built around how these businesses operate and how their customers search.

Setup & Accounts

About 5 minutes. You connect your Google and Facebook accounts through OAuth (the same "Sign in with Google" flow you use for other services). No API keys, no developer setup, no onboarding calls required. The system starts pulling data as soon as your accounts are connected.

Yes, always. GroMore connects to YOUR Google and Facebook accounts through OAuth. We never create accounts on your behalf or ask for your passwords. If you cancel GroMore, your accounts, campaigns, data, and everything else remain exactly where they are. Nothing changes on Google or Facebook's side.

We use OAuth 2.0 for all platform connections, which means we never see or store your Google or Facebook passwords. Data is transmitted over HTTPS. You can revoke GroMore's access from your Google or Facebook account settings at any time, independently.

You will need at least a Google Ads account to get the most value from GroMore. If you do not have one, creating a Google Ads account is free (you only pay when ads run). Same with Facebook. We recommend setting these up first, then connecting them to GroMore. If you need help, reach out and we can point you in the right direction.

Features & Capabilities

Google Ads, Google Analytics 4 (GA4), Google Search Console, Facebook/Meta Ads, Facebook Pages, and Google Drive. All through secure OAuth connections.

The system analyzes your performance data and generates specific, actionable suggestions. Not generic tips like "try improving your quality score." Specific actions like "pause the 'emergency plumbing near me' keyword in Campaign B because it has spent $340 with zero conversions this month, and reallocate that budget to the 'water heater repair' ad group which is converting at $28/lead." The AI suggests, you decide whether to act.

No. GroMore generates campaign plans and recommendations, but you trigger every action. When you create a new campaign through GroMore, it launches in a paused state by default. You review it, verify the targeting and budget, and then enable it yourself. Nothing spends money without your explicit decision.

Yes. GroMore includes a Facebook post scheduler. You can compose posts individually or upload a batch via CSV. Attach images from your Google Drive, pick a publish date and time, and schedule. You can view all scheduled posts and cancel any before it publishes.

Every action taken on your campaigns through GroMore is logged: what changed, when, and the before/after state. Campaign launches, budget changes, keyword additions, negative keywords, ad copy edits, everything. When something works (or breaks), you can trace exactly what happened. No more "we made some optimizations" without specifics.

No. $449/month covers every feature: reporting, AI recommendations, campaign tools, post scheduler, audit trail, all integrations, and email support. There are no premium tiers, no bolt-on features, and no usage-based charges.
