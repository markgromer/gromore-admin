Setup & Security

Most businesses are fully connected in under 15 minutes. You authorize your Google and Facebook accounts through secure OAuth, and GroMore pulls in your data automatically.

GroMore connects via OAuth, Google and Facebook's official authorization method. You grant specific permissions and can revoke them at any time. We never see or store your passwords.
