Connected Dashboard

All Your Data, One Screen

GroMore connects to your Google Ads, GA4, Search Console, and Facebook Ad accounts through secure OAuth. No API keys to manage, no manual data entry.

The dashboard shows your key performance indicators side by side: spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, cost per lead, and month-over-month trends.

  • Google Ads, GA4, and Search Console in one view
  • Facebook Ads and Page metrics alongside Google
  • Google Drive integration for ad creative and images
  • Month-over-month comparisons with trend arrows

Reporting & Insights

Reports That Actually Tell You What to Do

Monthly PDF reports from agencies exist to justify their fee, not to help you. GroMore reporting is different: every metric comes with context, and every section ends with specific, prioritized next steps.

If your cost per lead jumped 18%, the report explains why and tells you what to try next.

  • Month-over-month: spend, CPC, CPL, conversion rate
  • AI-generated recommendations ranked by impact
  • Plain language, no jargon - built for business owners
  • Google and Facebook in one unified report

Campaign Manager

Plan, Review, Then Launch

GroMore generates campaign plans based on structured templates proven for local service businesses. The system suggests campaign structure, ad groups, keywords, ad copy, and budgets.

You review the full plan before anything goes live. New campaigns start paused by default. You enable them on your schedule.

  • Niche-specific templates (HVAC, roofing, plumbing, etc.)
  • Keywords, negative keywords, ad copy generation
  • Campaigns launch paused by default for safety
  • Budget recommendations based on service area
  • Full audit log of every change

Post Scheduler

Schedule Facebook Posts Without Leaving the Platform

Organic reach still matters for local businesses. Write posts individually or upload a full month of content via CSV. Attach images from your Google Drive, pick a date and time, done.

  • Single post composition with image attachments
  • Bulk scheduling via CSV upload
  • Google Drive image picker built in
  • Calendar view of scheduled and published posts
  • Cancel any scheduled post before publish time

Audit Trail

Every Change, Documented

"We made some optimizations." What optimizations? When? Why? With agencies, you never find out until something goes wrong.

GroMore logs every action. Campaign launches, budget changes, keyword additions, ad copy edits. Date, time, who did it, and the before/after state.

  • Timestamped log of every modification
  • Before/after state for budget and bid changes
  • Exportable history for your records
  • Full visibility, no hidden "optimizations"
