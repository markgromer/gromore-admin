About GroMore

Built by Operators, for Operators

GroMore started because local businesses deserve the same calibre of ad technology that enterprise companies use, without the enterprise price tag.

The Origin

We Saw the Problem Firsthand

Every local service business faces the same dilemma: you need a steady pipeline of leads to grow, but the tools to get them are built for large brands with marketing departments.

You have two options - learn Google Ads yourself (takes hundreds of hours and thousands of dollars in trial and error), or hire an agency (costs $1,200 - $2,000/month and you never really know what they are doing).

We built GroMore to be a third option. A platform that connects your actual ad accounts, shows you real data, generates actionable recommendations, and lets you execute changes directly. No middleman, no black box, no guesswork.

Our Values

What We Stand On

🔑

Ownership

Your ad accounts are yours. Your data is yours. Your campaigns are yours. We connect to your platforms through secure OAuth so you always keep full control. If you cancel, everything stays in your accounts exactly as it is.

🔍

Transparency

Every action is logged. Reports are generated from your actual data, not curated snapshots. When you see a number in your GroMore dashboard, it matches what is in your Google Ads account.

🎮

Control

Campaigns launch paused by default. Recommendations are suggestions, not edits made behind your back. You review, you approve, you launch. Nothing changes without your say-so.
