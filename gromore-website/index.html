The Problem

Most Agency Setups Are Broken

Agencies charge $900 to $2,500/month. You get a PDF report full of jargon, no idea what changed, and zero control over your own accounts.

💰

Expensive for What You Get

A junior account manager with dozens of clients tweaks a few bids and sends a report you can not parse. That is what $1,500/month buys.

🚫

Zero Visibility

"Impressions are up 23%!" - great, but how many calls did it generate? Most reports are designed to look busy, not to inform decisions.

🔒

They Own Your Accounts

Your ad accounts, landing pages, tracking pixels, data. Try to leave and you start from zero. The dependency is by design.

The Solution

One Connected System, From Data to Action

GroMore closes the loop between reporting and execution. Data flows in, insights come out, and when you are ready to act, the tools are right there.

🔌

Connect Your Accounts

Link Google Ads, GA4, Search Console, Facebook, and Google Drive. One-click OAuth. Takes about 5 minutes. You own everything.

📈

Clear, Actionable Reporting

Month-over-month comparisons in plain English. What changed, what worked, and a prioritized list of what to do next. No vanity metrics.

💡

AI-Generated Recommendations

The system analyzes your data and suggests specific actions: adjust bids, pause underperformers, target new keywords. You decide what to act on.

🎯

Campaign Planning & Launch

Generate campaign plans from structured templates. Review the plan, then launch directly. New campaigns start paused by default for safety.

📅

Facebook Post Scheduler

Schedule organic Facebook posts one at a time or in bulk via CSV upload. Attach images from your Google Drive. Facebook handles delivery.

📝

Full Audit Trail

Every campaign change is logged: what was modified, when, and by whom. Makes accountability simple and post-mortems painless.

How It Works

Live in 5 Minutes. First Report in 24 Hours.

No onboarding calls. No 3-week setup. Connect your accounts and the system starts pulling data immediately.

1

Connect

Link your Google and Meta accounts through secure OAuth. One click per platform. Your credentials stay with Google and Facebook, not us.

2

Pull Data

The system pulls performance data from Google Ads, GA4, Search Console, and Facebook Ads. Merges it into a single, normalized dataset.

3

Review & Plan

Get month-over-month reports with clear recommendations. When you are ready, generate a campaign plan using proven templates for your niche.

4

Execute

Launch campaigns, adjust budgets, add negative keywords, schedule posts. Every action is logged. Nothing runs without your explicit trigger.
