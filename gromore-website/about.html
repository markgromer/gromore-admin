About

Built by an Operator, Not a Sales Team

GroMore exists because the agency model is broken for local service businesses. We built the platform we wished existed when we were managing accounts ourselves.

The Story

Why GroMore Exists

For years, local service businesses have had two choices for digital advertising: hire an agency that charges $1,500/month and gives you a PDF you can not understand, or try to figure out Google Ads and Facebook on your own with no guidance.

Both options are bad. Agencies create dependency on purpose. They run your ads through their accounts, lock you into long contracts, and provide just enough reporting to justify their fee. DIY means spending hours in Google's interface making expensive mistakes.

GroMore is the third option. A system that connects to your accounts (you own them, always), generates reporting in plain English, tells you specifically what to do next, and gives you the tools to act on those recommendations without needing a certification in digital marketing.

We built this platform because we managed Google and Facebook campaigns for home services companies and saw the same problems over and over: no transparency, no accountability, no control for the business owner paying the bills.

What We Believe

Principles, Not Buzzwords

You Own Your Accounts

Your Google Ads account, Facebook Ads account, analytics data, tracking pixels, landing pages. All yours, always. GroMore connects to them, it does not replace them. If you cancel, nothing changes on the platform side.

Transparency Over Everything

Every change is logged. Every report explains what happened and why. No "we made some optimizations" without telling you exactly what was optimized. If we can not explain it in plain English, we have not done our job.

Control, Not Autopilot

The system suggests, you decide. Campaigns launch paused so you can review before anything spends money. You trigger every action. AI generates recommendations, but a human (you) makes the calls.

Fair, Simple Pricing

One price, everything included. No setup fees, no contracts, no percentage-of-spend models where the platform profits from you spending more. $449/month whether your ad budget is $500 or $50,000.
