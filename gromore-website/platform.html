Platform

Everything You Need. Nothing You Don't.

One login, one system. Data ingestion, reporting, campaign planning, post scheduling, and a full audit trail. No bolt-on tools, no extra logins, no surprises.

Connected Dashboard

All Your Data, One Screen

GroMore connects to your Google Ads, GA4, Search Console, and Facebook Ad accounts through secure OAuth. No API keys to manage, no manual data entry.

The dashboard shows your key performance indicators side by side: spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, cost per lead, and month-over-month trends. Google and Facebook data sit next to each other so you can compare channel performance at a glance.

  • Google Ads, GA4, and Search Console in one view
  • Facebook Ads and Page metrics alongside Google
  • Google Drive integration for ad creative and images
  • Month-over-month comparisons with trend arrows

Reporting & Insights

Reports That Actually Tell You What to Do

Monthly PDF reports from agencies exist to justify their fee, not to help you. GroMore reporting is built differently: every metric comes with context, and every section ends with specific, prioritized next steps.

The system generates month-over-month comparisons for every key metric. If your cost per lead jumped 18%, the report explains why (which campaigns, which keywords, what changed in the auction) and tells you what to try next.

  • Month-over-month comparisons: spend, CPC, CPL, conversion rate
  • AI-generated recommendations ranked by impact
  • Plain language, no jargon - built for business owners
  • Google and Facebook performance in one unified report

Campaign Manager

Plan, Review, Then Launch

GroMore generates campaign plans based on structured templates proven for local service businesses. The system suggests campaign structure, ad groups, keywords, ad copy, and budgets based on your niche, service area, and goals.

You review the full plan before anything goes live. When you hit launch, new campaigns start paused by default. You enable them on your schedule, after verifying everything looks right.

  • Niche-specific campaign templates (HVAC, roofing, plumbing, etc.)
  • Keyword suggestions, negative keyword lists, ad copy generation
  • Campaigns launch paused by default for safety
  • Budget recommendations based on service area and competition
  • Full audit log of every change

Post Scheduler

Schedule Facebook Posts Without Leaving the Platform

Organic reach still matters for local businesses. GroMore includes a built-in Facebook post scheduler so you can plan your organic content right alongside your paid campaigns.

Write posts individually or upload a full month of content via CSV. Attach images from your Google Drive library, pick a date and time, and you are done. Facebook handles delivery. You can see all scheduled posts in a calendar-style view and cancel any post before it publishes.

  • Single post composition with image attachments
  • Bulk scheduling via CSV upload (content, date, time, links)
  • Google Drive image picker built in
  • Calendar view of scheduled and published posts
  • Cancel any scheduled post before publish time

Audit Trail

Every Change, Documented

One of the biggest problems with agencies: "We made some optimizations." What optimizations? When? Why? You never find out until something goes wrong.

GroMore logs every action taken on your accounts. Campaign launches, budget changes, keyword additions, negative keyword updates, ad copy edits, everything. Date, time, who did it, and what the state was before and after. When something works, you know exactly what changed. When something breaks, you can trace it back in seconds.

  • Timestamped log of every campaign modification
  • Before/after state for budget and bid changes
  • Exportable history for your records
  • Full visibility, no hidden "optimizations"
