# GroMore Platform - Complete Feature List

## CLIENT PORTAL

### Dashboard & Performance
- **Overall Health Grade** - A through F letter grade with numeric score showing business performance at a glance
- **Channel Performance Cards** - Individual scorecards for website traffic, Facebook ads, Google ads, organic social, and SEO
- **Performance Highlights** - AI-generated top 3 wins for the current period
- **Performance Concerns** - AI-generated top 3 issues that need attention
- **Month Selector** - View data for any reporting month

### KPI Tracking
- **KPI Dashboard** - Track target CPA, leads, and ROAS against goals
- **Plain-English Explanations** - Every metric explained in simple terms, not jargon
- **Color-Coded Status** - Visual indicators showing if KPIs are on track, at risk, or behind

### Action Plan (AI-Powered)
- **Prioritized Recommendations** - AI ranks what to do next based on data
- **Step-by-Step Instructions** - Each action includes exactly how to implement it
- **Difficulty Ratings** - 1 to 3 stars so users know what they can tackle themselves
- **Gamified Client Levels** - Progress from Rookie to Legend as actions are completed
- **Skill Categories** - Actions organized by Ad Optimization, SEO, Website Performance, Budget Strategy, Creative, Social, and Strategy
- **Dismiss and Restore** - Mark actions as done or irrelevant, bring them back if needed

### W.A.R.R.E.N. AI Assistant
- **Real-Time Chat** - Conversational AI assistant that knows the business and its data
- **Web Search** - Pull in live information from the web during conversations
- **Image Generation** - Create ad images and visuals with DALL-E
- **Long-Term Memory** - Saves and recalls important details across sessions
- **Chat History** - Full conversation history with option to clear
- **Proactive Suggestions** - Surfaces relevant tips based on current data
- **Model Selection** - Choose between GPT-4o-mini, GPT-4o, o3-mini, and others

### Campaign Management
- **Campaign List** - View all Google Ads and Meta campaigns in one place
- **Campaign Performance** - Real-time performance metrics per campaign
- **Pause/Resume Campaigns** - One-click campaign control
- **Budget Adjustment** - Change campaign budgets in real time
- **Negative Keywords** - Add negative keywords to Google Ads campaigns directly
- **Change Audit Log** - Full record of every campaign change made through the platform

### Campaign Creation & Launch
- **Quick-Launch Shortcut** - Answer 5 questions and get a ready-to-launch campaign
- **Strategy Selection** - Choose from predefined strategies (Omnipresent, Lead Gen, Hyper-Local, Retargeting for Meta; Search RSA, Display, Pmax, Video for Google)
- **AI Campaign Plan** - AI generates the full campaign plan based on strategy and business data
- **Configuration Form** - Review and customize every detail before launch
- **Image Upload** - Attach creative assets to campaigns
- **Draft Save/Load** - Save campaigns as drafts, come back and edit later
- **Preflight Checks** - Automated validation before launch catches issues early
- **One-Click Launch** - Launch directly to Google Ads or Meta from the platform

### Ad Builder (AI-Powered)
- **Google Search RSA Generation** - AI writes responsive search ad headlines and descriptions
- **Google Display Ad Generation** - Display ad copy and sizing
- **Google Performance Max Generation** - Pmax asset generation
- **Google Video Ad Generation** - Video ad scripts and copy
- **Meta Feed Ad Generation** - Facebook/Instagram feed ad copy
- **Meta Stories Ad Generation** - Stories-format ad creation
- **Multiple Variations** - 3 to 5 copy variations per ad type
- **Audience Recommendations** - AI suggests targeting based on business data
- **Industry-Specific Patterns** - Ad hooks tuned for plumbing, HVAC, dental, legal, and more
- **Ad Quality Scoring** - Each generated ad gets a quality score with explanation
- **Implementation Instructions** - Step-by-step guide for manual launch if needed

### Google Business Profile
- **Profile Data Viewing** - See all GBP information in one place
- **Completeness Score** - 0 to 100% audit of profile completeness
- **Missing Fields Checklist** - Exactly what's missing and needs to be filled in
- **Recent Reviews Display** - Latest customer reviews shown in the portal
- **Comprehensive GBP Audit** - Full analysis of what to improve

### Local Rank Heatmap Scanner
- **Geographic Grid Visualization** - See rankings mapped across a geographic area
- **Keyword Ranking Across Grid Points** - How a keyword ranks at different locations
- **Grid Configuration** - Customize grid size and search radius
- **Heatmap Results** - Color-coded visual showing ranking strength by location
- **Average Rank Calculation** - Overall ranking score across all grid points
- **Scan History** - View past scans and track changes over time

### Competitor Intelligence
- **Competitor Tracking** - Add and manage a list of competitors
- **Google Places Data** - Pull competitor ratings, review counts, address, hours
- **Meta Ad Library Scraping** - See competitors' active Facebook/Instagram ads
- **Website Analysis** - Automated competitor website review
- **Auto-Refresh Cache** - Competitor data refreshes every 7 days
- **Manual Refresh** - Force a fresh pull of competitor data anytime

### Creative Canvas / Design Studio
- **Ad Template Library** - Browse and use pre-built ad templates
- **Visual Canvas Editor** - Draw, add text, shapes, and images
- **Format Selection** - Templates for Facebook feed, Instagram Stories, and other formats
- **Template Save/Load** - Save custom templates for reuse
- **Template Export** - Export finished designs

### Post Scheduler
- **Social Post Scheduling** - Schedule organic posts to Facebook
- **Calendar View** - Visual calendar of all scheduled posts
- **Bulk Scheduling** - Schedule multiple posts at once
- **Message Editor** - Write and edit post copy
- **Image Upload** - Attach images to scheduled posts
- **URL Links** - Add link attachments to posts
- **Status Tracking** - See which posts are pending, published, or failed
- **Error Logging** - Detailed error info if a post fails to publish

### Google Drive Integration
- **File Browser** - Browse the brand's Google Drive folder
- **Subfolder Navigation** - Navigate through organized subfolders
- **File Upload** - Upload files directly to Drive
- **File Download** - Download files from Drive
- **Image Preview** - Thumbnail previews for image files
- **Auto-Folder Creation** - Automatically creates Creatives, Ads, Images, and Reports folders
- **Asset Selection** - Pick Drive images for use in the ad builder

### Logo Management
- **Logo Upload** - Upload brand logos
- **Multiple Variants** - Store different logo versions (light, dark, icon, etc.)
- **Primary Logo Selection** - Set which logo is the main one
- **Logo Renaming and Deletion**

### Brand Profile Settings
- **Brand Voice** - Define the brand's tone and communication style
- **Active Offers** - Current promotions and deals
- **Target Audience** - Describe ideal customers
- **Competitor List** - Track competitors directly from settings
- **Service Area** - Geographic coverage
- **Primary Services** - Core service offerings
- **Business Goals** - Strategic objectives
- **Reporting Notes** - Custom notes included in reports

### Client Settings
- **AI Model Selection** - Choose default AI model
- **Model Override Per Purpose** - Different models for chat, images, analysis, and ad building
- **API Key Override** - Use a custom OpenAI key
- **Google Drive Management**

### Help & Support
- **Help Page** - FAQs and platform guidance
- **Client Setup Guide** - Step-by-step getting started instructions

### Coaching System
- **Step-by-Step Guides** - Educational walkthroughs
- **Interactive Coaching Sessions** - Guided learning
- **Task Walkthroughs** - How-to instructions for specific tasks

### Feedback System (Beta)
- **Feedback Submission** - Report bugs, request features, share thoughts
- **Category Tagging** - Tag feedback as bug, feature, general, UI, or performance
- **Star Rating** - 1 to 5 rating with each submission
