This page is intended for Meta platform compliance and user data deletion requests related to GroMore Media integrations.

If you connected your Facebook or Meta account to GroMore Media and want your associated data removed, email privacy@gromoremedia.com with the subject line Meta Data Deletion Request.

Please Include

Your request should include the email address used for the connected account, the business or brand name associated with the connection, and any relevant Facebook Page ID or ad account ID if available. This helps us identify the correct records and avoid deleting the wrong workspace.

What We Delete

When a valid request is confirmed, we remove stored Meta connection tokens and related account access from our systems. Upon request, we also delete stored lead and messaging data associated with the requesting account where operationally and legally permitted.

Response Timing

We aim to acknowledge deletion requests within 5 business days and complete verified requests within 30 days unless a longer period is required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention reasons.

Need Account Disconnection Only?

If you only want to stop the integration, you can disconnect Meta from the GroMore settings area and remove the app from your Facebook Business integrations. A full deletion request is only needed when you want stored data removed from GroMore systems.
