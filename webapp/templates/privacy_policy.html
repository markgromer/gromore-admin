GroMore Media provides reporting, advertising operations, lead management, and messaging automation software for business clients. This Privacy Policy explains what information we process, why we process it, and how users can request access, correction, or deletion.

Information We Process

Depending on the integrations and features a client enables, GroMore may process account profile information, ad platform performance data, analytics data, search data, lead contact details, message transcripts, uploaded creative assets, and operational settings needed to run the service.

How We Use Data

We use connected platform data to generate reports, monitor marketing performance, manage inbound leads, automate qualifying conversations, export materials to connected storage destinations, and support account troubleshooting. We do not sell connected platform data.

Data Sharing

Data is shared only with service providers and integrations necessary to deliver the product, such as Google, Meta, hosting providers, email delivery providers, and other explicitly connected services. Access is limited to authorized staff and the client account connected to the data.

Retention

We retain account data for as long as needed to operate the product, satisfy legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Clients may request deletion of connected data and account records by contacting us.

User Rights

Clients and end users may request access, correction, export, or deletion of data associated with their account or lead records by emailing privacy@gromoremedia.com. We may verify identity before fulfilling requests.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect stored data, including authenticated access controls and secure transport for supported integrations. No internet service can be guaranteed fully secure, so users should avoid transmitting unnecessary sensitive information through connected messaging channels.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@gromoremedia.com.
