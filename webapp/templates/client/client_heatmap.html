How It Works
  • Set the keyword, radius, and odd grid size you want to test
  • Drag the scan center pin or click the map to move the grid
  • We query Google around every grid point and match your listing by Place ID first when available
  • Green means top 3 in the local pack, yellow means top 10 in the local pack, red means lower local-pack visibility, and gray means Places did not return you
  • The highlighted cell is the grid point closest to the live scan center
  • Service-area businesses can rank on page one organically while still being inconsistent in Places local-pack responses
